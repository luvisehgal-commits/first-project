pyaar tha, ya ek khamoshi ka sauda,
tumhari hasi ke peeche chhupa tha, bas udaasi ka jhootha vaada.
har pal tumhari yaad se dil bhar jaata,
aur khushiyon ke pal bhi bas aansuon mein dhal jaate.

tum chale gaye, saath le gaye har khushi ka rang,
sirf khaali saaye aur khamoshi ka sang.
har dhadkan ab tumhari doori ka gungun hai,
har saans mein bas ek khamosh shikayat hai.

ye zakhm, ye yaad, kabhi dhundhla nahi hote,
bas andar ke tukde, chhup kar cheekhte rehte.
aur main, ek bheega hua dil,
sirf khamoshi ke saaye mein jeeta hoon.

raat ke andhere mein, khud se baatein karte karte,
har yaad ek chhupa hua raaz khol jaati hai.
lagta hai, dil ka har tukda tumhare saath gaya,
aur main sirf ek khamosh saaya hoon, khud ke andar basa.

ye dard, jo lafzon se paray hai,
woh sirf insaan hone ka sabse gehra saboot hai.
har aansu, har khamoshi, har yaad ka bojh,
batata hai ki jeena bas dhadkan ke saath seekha jaata hai.

aur phir bhi, insaan chhupke se muskurata hai,
kyunki dil ke andar abhi bhi kuch pyaar ka sahara hai.
